## ğŸ¯ Interactive States

### Button States

#### Join Queue Button
- **Default**: Green background, white text, shadow
- **Hover**: Slightly darker green, larger shadow
- **Disabled**: 50% opacity, gray cursor
- **Loading**: Spinner icon, "Joining..." text

#### Leave Queue Button
- **Default**: Red border, red text, white background
- **Hover**: Light red background
- **Loading**: Spinner icon, "Leaving..." text

### Card States

#### Player Card
- **Normal**: White background, gray border
- **Current User**: Primary border, shadow, slight scale up
- **Hover**: Light shadow increase

#### Queue Card
- **Normal**: White background, gray border
- **Hover**: Primary border tint, shadow increase, scale 101%
- **Active**: Green accent on status badge
